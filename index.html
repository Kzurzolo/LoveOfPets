About Us

We work hard everyday to keep pets with their families, because they're family, too! 

 

We are working passionately to keep pet owners and their family pets together. I have seen numerous animals that have been found discarded on a country road, alone and afraid. While it may be heartbreaking, the sad fact is, many just don't make it. In the last two years I have seen a huge increase in this gut-wrenching tragedy. In many cases, pet owners have tried to turn their pet(s) over to animal shelters only to be told they are already overcrowded, and can't take in the beloved animal, or find it a new home. Pet owners don't know what to do so they tie them up at the shelter during the night, or drop them off on a roadside. In many instances, these are people who love their pets but have lost their jobs and homes.

They are depressed, scared and hurting, and don't know what to do. All they know is that they can't take care of the pet that they love, the very one that they had made a promise to keep safe 'til death do them part. They feel they have no other options left to them. This is where we come in. If we can get enough grants, enough donations; we can keep these family pets with the family that loves them, and shouldn't ever have to give them up. We can make sure that seniors don't have to choose between feeding themselves properly, or feeding their beloved dog, Toby. We can make sure that little Julie or Bobby are able to keep the dog that has been their best friend for 5, 7, or even 10 years of their lives. We can help cases where pets are put to death, simply because families can't afford a simple surgery. We can do this. And with your help, your commitment, we WILL do this.

Things we try and help with:

  • Seniors will never have to give up eating to take care of what, sadly enough, are sometimes their only companions. Make no mistake, this does happen. I have been behind seniors in the grocery store when they have had carts filled mostly with pet foodâ€¦and only peanut butter for themselves.
  • Family pets will no longer have to face death by euthanasia as an alternative to veterinary care that many cannot afford or medications that their family pet may need.
  • Creating a safety net for all families so they can feel secure in committing to and loving their pets, knowing their family now has a way to keep them in the event of unemployment or other economic disasters.
  • Alleviating the horrendous overcrowding of our State, City and County shelters.
  • People who suffer from Foreclosure should NOT have to lose their family pet because they can not locate a Pet Friendly new home, temporary boarding or afford a pet security deposit for their new place. FLP is putting plans into place to assist with that as well.
  • In the near future FLP plans to have in place a program to further assist people as well as shelters, by helping with the costs of adoption. This will allow so many more to open their homes to pets that otherwise will live out their lives in cages.

Our Goal

We at For The Love of Pets are pet owners ourselves, who have seen a problem and are working hard to make a difference. We are here to help provide assistance for those who are hard on their times, to help care for their pets, because they're family too. In this way, we work to keep pets in their homes, so that they are not put into shelters that are already overcrowded. This way, we help keep families together and happier, and shelters less crowded.

Animal Stories

Kisses

Kisses is the companion to a seriously ill little girl. Kisses developed soft tissue tumors (2) of them, that had to be removed. For The Love Of Pets paid for the surgery, gladly. Kisses will go on to live a very long, very loved life.

Jackie

Jackie received donatioin to get the bump on her head removed.

Stevie

Meet Stevie who had escaped from her home when company came over. After 3 frantic days, she was found. Stevie was in critical condition and the Vet fought for a week to save Stevie's back leg. The battle was lost due to a severe infection so the leg was removed to save Stevie's life. Stevie is now living a perfectly fine life and is happy, healthy, safe & loved. Stevie is also down to 8 lives and she knows this

Nova

She was viciously attacked in her own yard by a stray who jumped the fence.

Ivy and Opie

We need donations -and it's urgent. Ivy and Opie shown below need to be boarded. They are victims of domestic abuse and we have stepped in to do our damnedest to keep this little family together, with their fur babies. But it takes donations and we are praying for your help

Caramel Corn

Meet Caramel Corn. She was a poor gal used to breed pedigree babies to sell. Once she got mid aged, 5yrs, she was no longer useful. We have found the perfect spot for her to live out the rest of her years with a senior rescue rabbit buddy.

Snickers

She was hit by a car. Her owners didn't have the money for her surgery so she was about to be put to sleep to end her pain when a friend found us on Facebook and contacted us. We stepped in to help and Snickers is now living the good life in sunny California.
